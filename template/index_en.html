Loonone

After several nights out and partying until morning, the founders of LoonOne were looking for a simple and easygoing solution to connect with friends and nightlife enthusiasts. With this mission to bring outgoing people to the hot spots, questions, such as where to go or when to catch the next happy hour, are history. LoonOne is a platform that provides all the information you need for the night, connects you with your friends, and ensures a perfect nightlife experience for you!
